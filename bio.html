Hello, New York!




Since I was a child, I have been deeply interested in the functioning of the New York City Transit network. The first time I rode the New York City Subway, I became fascinated with how the system could be enhanced. Following the budget crisis in June 2010, I started devoting additional time to exploring the possibility of redesigning the subway map. I was inspired to create my second website by studying Computer Engineering Technology in college and working towards a Certificate of Completion in Front-End Web Development. About three years after building my art website, I considered bringing a website about how I think our transit system could improve into existence.
